## Who we are?
Ascentia is a biotechnology company combining internal research and development with Contract Research Organization (CRO) services.

We specialize in the design, synthesis, and evaluation of small molecules, supporting biotechnology companies, pharmaceutical organizations, and academic groups across all stages of drug discovery — from early research to lead optimization and scale-up.

Our interdisciplinary expertise spans medicinal chemistry, organic synthesis, molecular biology, and computer-aided drug design, enabling flexible, science-driven solutions tailored to diverse R&D needs.

In parallel with client projects, we develop our own research programs, including a growing library of structurally diverse, C(sp³)-rich building blocks.
